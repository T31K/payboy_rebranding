Why Payboy?

Built with People.

Payboy is driven by the HR issues businesses face, the feedback on our solutions, and a future where businesses can focus solely on bringing out the best in their people.

With various support avenues in place, we take feedback very seriously, to build something that our users love.



Built for People.

Payboy's intuitive platform is built for both employees and employers, to be used for digital submission of claims or quick check of payslip on the go or intensive planning work at the desk.

Payboy plays well with Xero, Financio and other app integrations that you may already be using.
